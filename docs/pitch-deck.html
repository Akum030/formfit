Meet FitSenseAI

A Live Agent that acts as your personal trainer — watching, listening, and speaking corrections using Gemini 2.5 Flash + Sarvam AI voice.

📷

Sees Your Form

MoveNet Thunder detects 17 body keypoints at 15+ FPS, entirely in-browser with TensorFlow.js. No data leaves your device for pose inference.

📐

Scores Every Rep

Custom angle-constraint engine computes joint angles in real time, scores 0–100% per rep phase, and shows live skeleton overlay with color-coded feedback.

🎙️

Speaks Hindi

Sarvam AI STT (hi-IN) understands your questions. Gemini generates coaching. Sarvam TTS (bulbul:v2) speaks it back — all in Hindi by default.

🚨

Interrupts Mid-Rep

When form score drops below 40%, the coach immediately interrupts with a correction — "Arre bhai! Ghutne bahar rakho!" — just like a real trainer.

Form Scoring

Joint angles compared against per-exercise, per-phase biomechanical constraints. Blended score 0–100%.

Coaching Engine

Rate-limited proactive triggers: rep feedback (every rep), low form (score < 60), urgent interruption (score < 40).

Language Switch

Toggle between 🇮🇳 Hindi and 🇬🇧 English at any time. Language flows through WebSocket URL param to all services.

Live Agent Voice Pipeline

The Voice Agent

Always-listening duplex voice agent. No push-to-talk. Speak naturally mid-exercise. The agent also speaks to you — and interrupts itself if you start talking.

What you can say

🇮🇳 "Bhai meri form kaisi hai?"
🇮🇳 "Kitne rep bacha hai?"
🇬🇧 "Is my squat deep enough?"
🇬🇧 "Start the next set"

What the coach says

✅ Good form
"Wah bhai! Perfect squat — aur ek!"
⚠️ Form warning
"Ghutna thoda aur bahar rakho"
🚨 Urgent interrupt
"Arre bhai! Ruk! Peet seedhi rakh!"
📊 Rep count
"Rep 8 complete! Score 87 percent"
